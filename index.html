About Me

Hi there! I'm Sandra, an aspiring Project Manager with a background in IT and executive support. Driven by a lifelong curiosity and a love for technology my goal is to harness my technical skills to create innovative and impactful solutions to businesses.

In my personal life my passions are my family, furniture restoration and interior design, along with food and wine.
